FileBoolean - OR

Logical OR of two file booleans. The result is true if at least 1 of the booleans is true.

Syntax

FILEBOOLEAN or FILEBOOLEAN
FILEBOOLEAN | FILEBOOLEAN
FILEBOOLEAN || FILEBOOLEAN

Example

FileSelect
  Size(10000000,0) or LastAccess("","1 month ago")
FileActions
  ...
FileEnd
