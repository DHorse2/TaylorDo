VolumeBoolean - OR

Logical OR of two volume booleans. The result is true if at least 1 of the booleans is true.

Syntax

VOLUMEBOOLEAN or VOLUMEBOOLEAN
VOLUMEBOOLEAN | VOLUMEBOOLEAN
VOLUMEBOOLEAN || VOLUMEBOOLEAN

Example

VolumeSelect
  Mounted(yes) or Writable(yes)
VolumeActions
  ...
VolumeEnd
